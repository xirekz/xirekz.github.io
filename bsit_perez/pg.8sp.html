What is storage platform?

Storage platform

CSPT

Is a method of saving data online rather than on your local computer. Uploading, editing, and sharing documents, presentations, spreadsheets, images, and other files with others requires an internet connection. Internet storage service providers do not save any data on your computer's hard drive..

Different storage platform

    Local storage:

  • Local storage, which refers to storing data on your device's hard drive or any other physical storage device such as external hard drives, USB drives, CDs, DVDs, etc., has several advantages:

    • Security:

  • Local storage provides more security than cloud storage because your data is stored on your device and not on a remote server that can be accessed by hackers.

    • Control:

  • With local storage, you have complete control over your data and can access it whenever you need it, without worrying about internet connectivity or reliance on a third-party service.

    • Speed:

  • Local storage provides faster access to data because it does not depend on internet speeds or network connectivity.

    • Cloud storage:

  • Cloud storage is a remote storage service that allows users to store and access data on a third-party server over the internet. Popular cloud storage platforms include Google Drive, Dropbox, Microsoft OneDrive.

    • Cloud storage has many benefits, some of which include:

    Accessibility:

  • Cloud storage allows you to access your files from anywhere in the world with an internet connection. You don't have to carry physical storage devices with you everywhere you go.

    • Scalability:

  • Cloud storage can grow or shrink based on your needs. You can easily add or remove storage space as needed.

    • Cost-effectiveness:

  • Cloud storage can be more cost-effective than traditional storage methods, as you only pay for the amount of storage you use.

    • Backup and Recovery:

  • Cloud storage provides a reliable backup and recovery solution. If your computer or device crashes, you can retrieve your files from the cloud.

    Security:

  • Cloud storage providers often offer robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or theft.

    • Overall, cloud storage provides a convenient, flexible, and secure way to store and access your files.
